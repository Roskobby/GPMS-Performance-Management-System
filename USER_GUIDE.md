## Introduction

### What is GPMS?

The General Performance Management System (GPMS) is a comprehensive digital platform for managing employee performance appraisals throughout the year.

### Key Features

- **Goal Setting**: Define and track annual performance goals
- **KPI Review**: Assess achievement of key deliverables (70% weight)
- **Behavioral Assessment**: Evaluate performance standards (30% weight)
- **Manager Review**: Line manager feedback and approval
- **PDF Export**: Generate professional appraisal reports
- **Workflow Management**: Status-based progression through review cycle

### Performance Model

GPMS uses a **70/30 performance model**:
- **70%**: Goals & KPI achievements
- **30%**: Behavioral standards and competencies

### Annual Cycle

1. **Beginning of Year**: Goal setting
2. **Throughout Year**: Continuous feedback
3. **End of Year**: KPI review, behavioral assessment, and final appraisal

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Go to Settings â†’ Change Password. You'll need your current password.

**Q: Can I edit goals after submitting?**  
A: No. Once submitted to your manager, goals are locked. Plan carefully before submitting.

**Q: When should I submit my goals?**  
A: Typically within the first month of the year. Check with your manager for exact deadlines.

**Q: What if I don't have 3 goals?**  
A: You can have fewer goals. Just focus on meaningful, impactful objectives.

### Employee Questions

**Q: How are weights calculated?**  
A: Automatically based on priorities. Higher priority = higher weight.

**Q: Can I add more than 3 deliverables per goal?**  
A: Yes, use the "Add Deliverable" button. There's no fixed limit.

**Q: What happens after I submit to my manager?**  
A: Your manager reviews, adds comments, and submits to HR. You'll see the final result.

**Q: How do I know my manager has reviewed my appraisal?**  
A: Status will change to ğŸŸ¢ "Submitted to HR" when complete.

### Manager Questions

**Q: Can I edit an appraisal after submitting to HR?**  
A: No. Review carefully before submitting. All changes are final.

**Q: What if an employee's submission is incomplete?**  
A: Contact the employee directly. They may need to withdraw and resubmit (if still in draft).

**Q: How do I export all my team's appraisals?**  
A: Open each review screen and click the PDF button. Save all PDFs to a folder.

**Q: Can I see other managers' team appraisals?**  
A: No. You can only access your direct reports for privacy and security.

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Cannot login  
**Solutions**:
- Check employee number (no spaces)
- Verify password (case-sensitive)
- Try password reset
- Contact HR/Admin

**Problem**: Forgot password  
**Solutions**:
- Contact HR/Admin for reset
- They can generate new password

### Submission Issues

**Problem**: "Submit to Manager" button not appearing  
**Solutions**:
- Save goals first using "Save Goals" button
- Ensure at least one goal has content
- Refresh the page

**Problem**: Cannot submit (validation error)  
**Solutions**:
- Fill in all required fields
- Check for empty descriptions
- Ensure priorities are set

### Performance Issues

**Problem**: Page loading slowly  
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Refresh the page

**Problem**: PDF not downloading  
**Solutions**:
- Check browser pop-up blocker
- Allow downloads in browser settings
- Try different browser
- Check PDF download folder
